  1. Consider the logistic function defined by the formula \[ \ell(x) = \frac{530}{1+8\mathrm{e}^{-0.02x}}\,. \]
    1. What is the domain and range of the function \(\ell\,?\)
    2. What is the value of \(\ell(200)\,?\) Write down an exact expression for the value, but also express the value as a decimal rounded to three decimal places.
    3. For what value(s) of \(x\) does \(\ell(x) = 100\,?\) Write down an exact expression for the value(s), but also express the value(s) as a decimal rounded to three decimal places.
  2. Consider the Gompertz function defined by the formula \[ g(x) = 530(0.1)^{\left(0.96^x\right)} \]

    3. For what value(s) of \(x\) does \(g(x) = 300\,?\) Write down an exact expression for the value(s), but also express the value(s) as a decimal rounded to three decimal places.
