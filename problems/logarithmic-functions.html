  2. Without appealing to technology, figure out the exact value of the following logarithms. (Hint: they’re all whole numbers.)

  4. Find the value(s) of \(x\) that satisfies each of these equations.
  5. If \(\log_7(x) = 5,\) what must the exact value of the following expressions be? (Hint: this is an exercise of your effective use of the “rules of logarithms”.)

  7. In May 2008 an earthquake of magnitude 6.8 struck Honshu, Japan, causing some injuries and building damage. In March 2011 a magnitude 9.0 earthquake stuck Honshu, killing thousands of people. How much more powerful was this second earthquake?
