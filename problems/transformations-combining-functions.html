Transformations of Graphs
& Combining Functions

  1. Without appealing to technology, describe how the graph of \(g(x) = \sqrt{x-1}+3\) compares to the graph of \(f(x) = \sqrt{x}.\)
  2. Let \(\gamma(x) = x^{3/2}\) and consider its graph \(y = \gamma(x).\) Suppose we want to shift this graph five to the left; how would the formula for \(\gamma\) have to change to cause this transformation of the graph?
