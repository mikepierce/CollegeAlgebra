Linear Systems

  1. (Pearson) Janet has $300,000 she'd like to invest. She's researched two different investments:
    • A money-market fund offered by her credit union guaranteeing a 6.5% annual return.
    • A high risk mutual fund that is projected to net 7.7% annual return.
    She wants to balance her investment between these two funds to get an expected return of 6.5%. How should should split up her principal into these two funds?
  2. (Pearson) Drug C and Drug R are two different drugs being prescribed to treat the same rare medical condition.
    1. Drug C had a 2.5% market share at the beginning of last year, but after an eleven week marketing campaign it shot up to 9.9% market share. Use this information to write a linear function that models the market share of Drug C as a function of the number of weeks after the beginning of last year.
    2. Drug R had a 8.1% market share at the beginning of last year, but after Drug C's marketing push, it dropped to 6.1% market share. Use this information to write a linear function that models the market share of Drug R as a function of the number of weeks after the beginning of last year.
    3. What week was it during the beginning of last year that the market share of Drug C overtook Drug R?
