    • Walt Disney Company (DIS), a large, reliable “blue chip” corporation with slow by steady growth which has seen long-term gains on average of about 1.8% annually over the last ten years (according to Forbes).
    • The Bancorp Inc (TBBK), the bank backing fintech companies Venmo and Chime, has had a wild year, its stock shooting up 13.78%!
    Disney is certainly a safer bet, but she notices that she can’t just invest all the money in DIS because 1.8% of $300,000 is only $5,400, so she has to split the investment between Disney and the riskier investment Bancorp. How should she split the $300,000 investment to get an expected return of $8,000?
